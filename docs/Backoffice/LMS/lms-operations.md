## 📘 LMS Operations

The Loan Management System (LMS) is responsible for managing active loans, tracking repayments, and communicating updates to LOS.
It ensures that all loan activities are accurately recorded and synchronized with the originations and backoffice systems.

Think of LMS as the operational engine of loan servicing.
![mermaid](/img/mermaid-diagram.png)

## 🔄 Key Responsibilities
### Repayment Tracking
* Monitors due dates, overdue payments, and completed repayments
* Maintains a record of all transactions per loan
### Loan Status Updates
* Updates the LOS with repayment statuses
* Marks loans as Active, Overdue, Defaulted, or Closed
### Notifications & Alerts
* Sends reminders for upcoming or missed repayments
* Notifies LOS and Backoffice if interventions are needed
### Audit & Reporting
* Logs all repayment actions and status changes
* Provides data for compliance, reconciliations, and reporting
