## 🔷 What is LOS?

The Loan Origination System (LOS) manages everything before a loan is created.

It focuses on:

Customer onboarding
Loan application intake
Document collection and verification
Credit assessment and approval

Once a loan is approved and finalized, the process moves forward.

## 🔷 What is LMS?

The Loan Management System (LMS) takes over after the loan is disbursed.

It handles:

Loan account creation
Repayment schedules
EMI tracking
Interest calculations
Penalties and overdue management
Loan closure

## ⚙️ Lifecycle Transition

A loan moves from LOS to LMS at a specific point:

Customer submits application (LOS)
Application is reviewed and approved (LOS)
Loan is disbursed ✅
Loan account is created (LMS)
Repayments and servicing begin (LMS)
