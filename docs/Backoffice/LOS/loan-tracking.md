## 📘 Loan Tracking

The Loan Tracking page provides a real-time view of all active loans.
It shows how the LOS and LMS work together to manage loan statuses, repayments, and operational updates.

This page is the bridge between origination and servicing — giving backoffice users full visibility.

## 🔄 How LOS & LMS Work Together
* LMS (Loan Management System): Tracks actual loan repayments, schedules, and balances.
* LOS (Loan Origination System): Receives updates from LMS about due payments, repayments made, and loan status changes.

The LOS syncs all statuses from LMS so users see a single source of truth for loan tracking.

## 📊 What You See on Loan Tracking

The page consolidates information from different entities in the lending ecosystem:

* Application Status → Pending, Approved, Disbursed, Closed
* Loan Status → Active, Overdue, Defaulted, Closed
* Repayment Tracking → Amount due, paid, and balance remaining
* Entity Updates → Notifications about actions from LOS or LMS

This allows backoffice users to monitor every loan from application to repayment.
