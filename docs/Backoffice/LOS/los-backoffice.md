## 📘 LOS Backoffice

The Backoffice acts as the brain of the Loan Origination System (LOS).
It is responsible for coordinating all communication, managing workflows, and ensuring all loan processes are properly tracked and synchronized.

Think of it as the control tower of the entire lending operation.

## 🔄 Key Responsibilities
### Workflow Orchestration
* Routes loan applications between different modules
* Ensures approvals, disbursements, and repayments follow the correct flow
### Status Tracking & Synchronization
* Maintains up-to-date loan and application statuses
* Syncs with LMS to track repayments and update LOS
### Role & Access Management
* Assigns responsibilities to different backoffice users
* Ensures only authorized personnel can approve loans, adjust products, or trigger disbursements
### Audit & Monitoring
* Records every action taken on loans and applications
* Supports compliance and reporting requirements
### Communication Hub
* Acts as the bridge between LOS modules, LMS, and other integrated systems
* Ensures all entities have accurate, real-time data
