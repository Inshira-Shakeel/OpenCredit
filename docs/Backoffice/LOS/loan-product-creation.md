## 📘 Loan Product Setup

The Product Setup page allows backoffice users to define and configure loan products.
You can either create a product from scratch or use predefined templates to save time.

A loan product determines:

* How customer loans behave
* How repayments are calculated
* How fees and interest are applied
* How transactions are posted into the system

Think of this as the blueprint for your lending offerings.

## 🏗️ Product Configuration Options

When setting up a loan product, you’ll configure:

### 1. Product Name & ID Allotment
* Unique identifier for easy reference
* Product name visible across LOS dashboards
### 2. Validity Dates
* Define start and end dates of product availability
### 3. Category
* Classify product type (e.g., personal loan, business loan, mortgage)
### 4. Loan Amount & Tenor
* Minimum and maximum loan amounts
* Repayment period / tenor
### 5. Repayment Rules
* Frequency, schedule, and methods
### 6. Disbursement Settings
* Include fees/interest deductions if needed
* Control partial or full disbursement
### 7. KYC Group
* Assign the KYC group applicable for this product
* Determines required documentation
### 8. Interest / Markup Setup
* Configure interest rates, markup rules, and calculation methods
### 9. Cooling-Off Period
* Define time window where customers can cancel or adjust
### 10. Event Fees
* Setup fees triggered by specific events (late payment, early closure)
### 11. Credit Scoring Rules
* Determine eligibility criteria based on credit score
### 12. Transactions & Rule Builder
* Configure which transaction types are allowed
* Build rules for automated actions, limits, and validations
### 13. GL Account Mapping
* Link product transactions to the general ledger accounts
* Ensures accurate accounting for fees, interest, and repayments
